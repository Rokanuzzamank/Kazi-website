Faith, Self-Love & Purpose

April 2025

I love reading and listening to the Quran, and spending time in prayer. These moments ground me and guide my heart. The best teachings from the Quran and the actions of the beloved Prophet (PBUH) encourage us to practice self-love and grow as individuals. It is our duty to care for our soul, our worth, and our purpose — for the sake of Allah (SWT).

“Allah does not burden a soul beyond that it can bear.”

This belief keeps me strong and reminds me that Islam is not just a religion, but a way of life filled with compassion, balance, and strength.
